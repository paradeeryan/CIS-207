Why Are Monkeys So Important To Us?

Our founder Fred beleives that monkeys are the origin of humanity, so it's no surprise that Fred takes such a liking to these furry creatures. We are more concerned about their well-being in the wildlife, becuase of the mass habitat desctruction throughout the world leaving many species to perish.

Our Mission

Our purpose is to preserve the habitats of monkeys in every habitat around the world. Unfortunatley, this is no easy task as it costs a lot of money and required a lot of people who share the same love for monkeys as Fred does. We do accept donations here to help fund the cause.
