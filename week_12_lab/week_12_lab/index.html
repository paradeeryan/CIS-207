Media Example

MP3 Audio Section

The audio tag supports 3 different audio tags, these are MP3, Ogg, and WAV.

MP4 Video Section

The video tag supports 3 formats, these are MP4, Ogg, and WebM

Make sure to include a poster for when the video is not playing. This is an image file but is stored with the media to keep things together

If your video includes speech, be sure to provide closed captions
